About Us

Building Champions Since 2010

Vaishali Education was founded with a singular vision — to provide world-class coaching for competitive and board exams right here in Muzaffarpur. Under the leadership of Manoj Sir, we have transformed thousands of students into toppers, doctors, and engineers.

Our methodology combines rigorous academics with personalised mentoring, ensuring every student receives the attention they deserve. We cover Class 8 to 12 under CBSE & Bihar Board curricula, with specialised tracks for JEE, NEET, NDA, and CUET.
